# Design System Document

## Theme: Horizon Dark

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of the "Horizon Dark" theme, designed for a modern, engaging digital experience.

---

### **Color Palette**

The Horizon Dark theme leverages a sophisticated dark mode, ensuring visual comfort and energy efficiency.

*   **Primary Color:** `#38BDF8` (A vibrant sky blue, ideal for highlighting key actions and interactive elements.)
*   **Secondary Color:** `#6afab4` (A refreshing mint green, providing a calm accent for less prominent UI components.)
*   **Tertiary Color:** `#1c5194` (A deep ocean blue, used for subtle highlights and decorative touches.)
*   **Neutral Color:** `#0F172A` (A dark slate, serving as the foundational background for surfaces and textual elements.)

---

### **Typography**

A carefully selected typographic scale ensures readability and visual hierarchy across all content.

*   **Headline Font:** `Space Grotesk` (For headlines, offering a modern and impactful visual.)
*   **Body Font:** `Inter` (For body text, chosen for its excellent legibility and versatility.)
*   **Label Font:** `Inter` (For labels and small text, maintaining consistency and clarity.)

---

### **Shape and Form**

The theme embraces a specific geometric style to define the visual characteristics of UI elements.

*   **Roundedness:** `1` (Subtle roundedness, providing a soft touch while maintaining a clean, modern aesthetic.)

---

### **Layout and Spacing**

The spatial arrangement of elements is crucial for clarity and user experience.

*   **Spacing:** `2` (Normal spacing, ensuring a balanced and comfortable layout with adequate whitespace between elements.)
